About the Firm

We are simple men

"The infrastructure abides. I don't know about you, but I take comfort in that."
— Jeff L., Chief Enterprise Officer

Evolved Services was founded in 1998 on the principle that enterprise infrastructure does not require complexity — it requires discipline, documentation, and the willingness to take a hard line when someone scuffs a drive.

We have operated mission-critical systems across industries that demand absolute reliability. We have seen organizations fall because they had no plan, no redundancy, and frankly, no rug.

We do not offer empty assurances. We offer architecture. And when something goes wrong — and it will — we are already watching.
