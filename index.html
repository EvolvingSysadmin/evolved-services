We are simple men

Evolved Services was founded on the principle that enterprise infrastructure does not require complexity — it requires discipline, documentation, and the willingness to take a hard line when someone scuffs a drive.

Our team has operated mission-critical systems across industries that demand absolute reliability. We have seen organizations fall because they had no plan, no redundancy, and frankly, no rug. We fix that.
